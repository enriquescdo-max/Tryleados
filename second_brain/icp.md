## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary: Life-Event Triggered Individuals
People experiencing a trigger event that creates an immediate insurance need:
- **New movers** — just signed a lease or bought a home, need coverage by move-in date
- **Car buyers** — purchasing from dealer or private party, need same-day auto coverage
- **New homeowners** — closing on property, lender requires coverage before funding
- **Apartment renters** — landlord requiring renters insurance as lease condition

### Secondary: Referral Partners (B2B)
Professionals who interact with our ICP daily and can refer warm leads:
- **Apartment locators** — Austin/Houston market, see renters daily (Smart City Apartment Locating)
- **Auto dealers** — finance managers who need buyers insured before driving off lot
- **Realtors/mortgage brokers** — buyers need homeowners insurance before closing
- **Property managers** — require renters insurance from all tenants
