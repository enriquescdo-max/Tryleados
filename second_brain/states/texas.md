# Texas Insurance Solicitation Rules

## Outreach Compliance
- Governed by Texas Department of Insurance (TDI)
- Must be licensed in TX to solicit P&C insurance
- TCPA applies to all outbound calls/texts — verbal consent required before dialing mobile numbers
- DNC registry check required before cold calling
- SB 942 (CA) does not apply to TX residents

## Required Disclosures
- Must identify yourself as a licensed insurance agent
- Must identify your agency name
- Must state purpose of call at outset

## Cold Email Rules
- CAN-SPAM applies — physical address in footer, unsubscribe mechanism required
- Do not represent coverage as "free" or use deceptive subject lines
- Cannot guarantee coverage before completing application

## TX-Specific Products
- State Farm, Allstate largely pulled back from TX home market (2022-2024)
- FAIR Plan available as last resort for uninsurable TX homes
- TWIA (Texas Windstorm Insurance Association) for Tier 1-2 coastal counties
- Flood: NFIP or private flood — separate policy always required
