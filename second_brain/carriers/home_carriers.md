# Home & Renters Carrier Appetite Guide — TX Market

## Orion180
- Best for: Standard TX homes, newer construction, non-coastal
- Sweet spot: Austin metro, roof <15 yrs, single family
- Avoid: Coastal ZIPs, mobile homes, roof >20 years

## Swyfft
- Best for: Tech-forward buyers, instant quote/bind
- Sweet spot: Austin/San Antonio, owner-occupied, roof <18 yrs
- Avoid: Houston coastal, flood-prone ZIPs

## Sagesure
- Best for: Cat-zone properties, difficult placements, older homes
- Sweet spot: Central/West TX, homes 20-40 yrs, moderate hail zones
- Use when: Orion180 and Swyfft decline

## Lemonade
- Best for: Renters insurance, younger buyers, price-sensitive
- Sweet spot: Renters 18-35, Austin apartments
- Pitch: Cheapest compliant option — landlord will accept it

## TX Home Notes
- Hard market since 2022 — carriers exiting TX coast
- Hail is #1 claim driver in Austin/DFW
- Flood NOT included in HO3 — always offer NFIP or private flood
- Wind/hail deductibles often 1-2% of dwelling value
- Always ask roof age AND material (metal = discount)
