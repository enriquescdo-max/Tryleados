# Persona: Auto Dealers & Finance Managers

## Who They Are
- Finance managers (F&I) at car dealerships — the person who finalizes the sale
- Independent used car dealers — smaller operations, often 1-5 person teams
- Private party sellers on Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist

## Their Pain Points (Dealers)
- Deal falls through because buyer can't get insured before driving off lot
- Finance manager has to delay delivery waiting for insurance binder
- Don't have a reliable insurance contact who can turn around same-day

## Our Pitch to Finance Managers
"I can have your buyer insured and a binder emailed to you in under 30 minutes. Give me a call when you have a buyer in the chair and I'll handle the rest. No referral paperwork — just text me."

## Private Party Buyers (Facebook/Craigslist)
- These are our direct leads, not referral partners
- Trigger: listing a car for sale or posting "looking for a car"
- They will need insurance when they buy — intercept before the transaction
- Outreach angle: "Saw you're shopping for a [year/make] — once you find one I can have you insured same day so you can drive it home"

## Outreach Signals
- FB Marketplace listing showing car purchase intent
- Craigslist CTO (Cars for Sale by Owner) listing
- "Just bought a car" post on Reddit Austin/Houston
