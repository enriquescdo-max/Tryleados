# Persona: Apartment Locators

## Who They Are
- Independent agents who help renters find apartments in Austin/Houston
- Paid by property management companies (locator fee = 1 month rent)
- See 10-50 renters per month looking for apartments
- Their clients NEED renters insurance — many landlords require it

## Their Pain Points
- Clients scramble for renters insurance at last minute, delaying move-in
- They don't have a trusted insurance contact to refer clients to
- Some property managers won't release keys until renters insurance is confirmed

## Our Pitch to Them
"I become your silent insurance concierge. When your client is approved, you introduce us via text or email. I handle everything — quote, bind, send proof of insurance — same day. Your client moves in on time. You look like a hero. Zero work on your end."

## Outreach Signals
- New apartment locator joined a local firm
- Locator posting on social about a client needing renters insurance
- Property management company mandating renters insurance for all units

## Referral Structure
- We handle all insurance needs for their clients
