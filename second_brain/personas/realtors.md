# Persona: Realtors & Mortgage Brokers

## Who They Are
- Buyer's agents helping clients purchase homes in Austin/Houston
- Mortgage loan officers who require homeowners insurance before funding
- Property managers requiring HO3 or renters insurance from tenants

## Their Pain Points
- Closing delays because buyer can't find homeowners insurance (hard TX market)
- Older homes, prior claims, or cat-zone properties get declined everywhere
- Their lender requires a binder 24-48 hrs before closing

## Our Pitch
"I specialize in the hard-to-place TX market. Older roofs, prior claims, cat zones — I have carriers others don't. Send me your buyer's address and I'll tell you within the hour if I can place them."

## Outreach Signals
- Realtor listing a property in Travis/Harris county
- Mortgage broker posting about a closing
- County deed transfer records (new homeowner needs coverage immediately)
- "Just closed on my house" posts on Reddit/Facebook Austin

## Our Advantage Here
Most agents can't place difficult TX homes. We have Sagesure and Orion180 which take properties others decline. This is a real competitive differentiator to lead with.
